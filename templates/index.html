New E.P. "Weekend At Barretts" out now!

June 1st, 2021

The new E.P. from The Takeouts titled "Weekend At Barretts" is now available to stream on all platforms!

Recorded at Noisy Neighbor Studios in Las Vegas, Nevada, The Takeouts look to introduce their new lead singer, Jake, the brother of The Takeouts' bassist Gavin. This short, but action packed E.P. was the culmination of stressful times and heartbreak.

Recording at Noisy Neighbor Studios gave The Takeouts amazing support from talented musicians in the Las Vegas area. Tracks that include drums were tracked by the extremely talented Nick Mendoza, who laid down the raddest of beats to three of the four tracks on the E.P. Jacob Mendoza, the brother of Nick, provided an other-worldy performance for the lead guitar work on the third track "Please".

"Weekend At Barretts" would have never been possible without Barrett himself. The up-and-coming producer/sound engineer made tracking this E.P. easier than ever imagined. Barrett operates his studio in a manner that most musicians can only ever wish to get out of a studio session. Without his attention to detail and knack for a sonic picture, this E.P would not exist, period.

Be on the lookout for new material from The Takeouts as they plan to release a full length album sometime in the later months of summer 2021. But for now you can listen to the new E.P. "Weekend At Barretts" with the link below!
